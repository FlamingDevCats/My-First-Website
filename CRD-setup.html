Installing Chrome Remote Desktop on your Computer

So this page will contain the instructions for installing Chrome Remote Desktop on your computer. One thing to note is that you will need administrator access. Other than that, it should be pretty hard to mess up imo. This is the most important part because this is the stuff that enables you to actually access your computer remotely.

  1. Go to your browser of choice.
  2. Go to remotedesktop.google.com or search for Chrome Remote Desktop and click the first link to Chrome Remote Desktop.
  3. Click Access my computer.
  4. If it asks to log into your google account, log into the google account we made on the previous page.
  5. It should present you with this. Click the download button.
  6. It will open a new window where you have to install the extension. If you can find it on the addon/extension store for your browser use that if it is from Google. You can close that window once you finish installing the extension.
  7. Once that finishes, It will begin downloading the installer. Save it somewhere if it prompts you to save it somewhere.
  8. Now run the installer. If you can't do that, then
    1. Click the download icon near the top-right corner of the browser window.
    2. Mouse over the newly downloaded file.
    3. Click the open icon.
  9. A new window will pop up. This is the installer. A UAC prompt will appear. Click Yes.
  10. Once that finishes installing (when the window closes), go back to the website. This will appear. Click Accept & Install.
  11. Now pick a name and then click next.
  12. Now pick a pin, re-enter it and click Start.
  13. A UAC prompt will appear. Click yes.

So now you can access your computer remotely. The access panel should look like this upon refresh or visiting the page again.
