Setting up a Google Account for Chrome Remote Desktop

Alright, so we will be setting up a google account exclusively for Chrome Remote Desktop. The reason why we need to setup an account exclusively for Chrome Remote Desktop is because your normal google account might have 2fa (two-factor authentication) and other things that might give you problems with logging into your google account using an unknown device and network, which is really sus to google's security.

I recommend using a during the setup process so when you use the same , so it will not block you from logging into the account because the ip is the same. Also, we should verify the google account as soon as possible so you don't get forced to verify the account in the middle of remote accessing your own computer. One more thing I recommend (if you even can) is making the account in school a . Let's begin now.


  1. Go to your browser of choice.
  2. Go to google.com
  3. If it says sign in at the top right corner (as shown below), click it. If it looks more like the image below, follow the 2 steps below.
    1. Click the profile at the top-right of the screen
    2. Then click add account.
  4. Now click Create Account
  5. Then click For my personal use
  6. Now fill in details. They do not have to be real. For the birth date, make sure it is not the age of a child, which is 12 or younger in the UK.
  7. If it asks you to verify the account, verify it.

Now we got an account exclusively for Chrome Remote Desktop. Next we will look at installing Chrome Remote Desktop.
